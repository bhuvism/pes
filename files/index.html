Why CodeGeeko

At CodeGeeko, we believe ANYONE can code, if taught in the right way. We are here to do it the right and real way.

Gamification

We motivate students to learn REAL TEXT BASED CODING by using game elements in learning environments. Our goal is to maximize enjoyment and engagement through capturing the interest of learners and inspiring them to continue learning.

Curriculum is in line with APCSP

AP CSP (Advanced Placement Computer Science Principles) spotlights how computing is changing the world, and provides ample opportunity for students from all backgrounds to connect abstract concepts to real-world implications of the field.
